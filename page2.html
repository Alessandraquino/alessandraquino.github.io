Poems about Love

"Love you through my Dreamscape"

My lenses focus on you

Through in-depth field

I love you through my point of view

I wake up and you always make me food

Spam and eggs

The way I like too

You make me smile and laugh

Tranquil feelings surround you

Happiness is around you

No matter the destination

We travel through communication

From time difference

To schedule conflicts
