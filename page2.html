Residue (2023) Acrylic on Mattboard, 20x20 inches

My intention behind 'Residue' is to represent my state of mind during a period of trust issues and self-isolation, the impact a person has left you with their baggage, and how it lingers onto you. With that experience it makes you reflect on those events that made you emotionally available and be able to open up again to people who are worth your time. Regardless, of your past it does not fully represent who you are in the present. What makes this piece difficult is how much I remember those specific events
