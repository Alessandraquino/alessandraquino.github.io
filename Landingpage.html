About the Artist

My name is Alessandra Aquino, I am a 21-year-old Filipino woman based in the Bay Area. I am currently at San Jose State University getting a BFA in Pictorial Art. My mediums are usually painting and illustrations. The topics I involve in my work are women's rights, politics, and self-identity as a Filipino-American in the US.
