Poems about Love

"March"

I loved you then

I love you now

As I fully realized how

From meeting in a gallery

To you becoming my home

I never knew how much I have grown

Now I don't ever feel alone

The love that you create

Makes me levitate

Another world

A better outlook

You put me in a good state

I knew it was never too late

All I had to do

Was just wait
