Poems about Love

"what are you"

I appreciate you

But I hate it here

I am conflicted

Why are you all so restricted

To tradition, to standard

I cannot believe you could even stand her

Toxicity, this city

So much catholicity

Will I ever be able to love this city?

Am I capable to connect again

Redirect my intellect yet

Be called whitewashed without affect

I'm Filipino, I swear I am

But I don't know if I can bear this dispair again
