Meme Mashup

For my Mashup I used Photoshop to use 3 key Memes, all are cat memes because I used to rescue cats during the time of the Pandemic. So I wanted to create a wholesome meme because I love them so much. So I created a date setting where 2 cats are going on a picnic date but they are disrupted by another black cat who keeps staring at the happy cat, ruining the intimate moment.

This was an extra side project where I created a pink cherry blossom island where all the cute memes live.
